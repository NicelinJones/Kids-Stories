DRAMA ARTIST AND HIS DIAMOND

Story Written By: Bro.Sam Jebadurai

Illustrated By: NicelinJones

Edited By: Jaiannah Jones

Once upon a time one Drama Artist from India went to the London for showing his talent. He showed his talent to the people, all peoples were mesmerized because he changed his make up every time. Suddenly he changed as a girl,suddenly he changed as a boy, and suddenly he changed as a old man. So peoples were mesmerized and they gave him a lot of money. He earned more money in London,nearly one crore.

He thought how to take this much of money to India. He decided to buy a Diamond with a value of one crore. So he bought a more beautiful Diamond with his money. He returned back to India.There was no flight in those days because Aeroplane was not found yet. So he decided to go by the ship for sea travel.Travelling in ship took more than a week. He thought,to show his talent in the ship.So he can also earn more money.He started to show his talent in the ship also. All peoples in the ship also saw his talent and they were amazed. At that time He thought to show his talent with his Diamond. So He took out his Diamond and showed that beautifullness. All the peoples saw with mouth opened. Then He throwed it up to the sky and catched it. It was very beautiful because rainbow colors came out from inside the Diamond. Peoples were mesmerized and amazed. So he tryed to throw the Diamond more higher to the sky. But Suddenly the ship shook and the Diamond fell down into the deep sea. No one could take it because it was very deep like two mountains deep. So Drama Artist fedup and went to his bed with a sad face.

The Moral

From this story what we learn is ,Drama Artist lose his valuable Diamond into the deep sea,like him we should not lose our soul. Because our soul is very important for everybody. If we do the sin and lose our soul into satan, our life is a waste. we have to keep the soul with out sin. Unfortunately or unkownly if we do the sin,the only way is Lord jesus. He is the one and only God, he who victory the satan.
