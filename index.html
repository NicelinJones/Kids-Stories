Welcome to the Story Time

Illustrated By: NicelinJones

Once upon a time one Drama Artist from India went to the London for showing his talent. He showed his talent to the people, all people are mesmerized because he changed his make up every time. Suddenly he changed as a girl,suddenly he changed as a boy,suddenly he changed as a old man. So peoples are mesmerized and they gave lot of money to him. He earned more money in London,nearly one crore.

He thought how to take this much of money to the India. He decided to buy a Diamond with a value of one crore. And he bought a more beautiful Diamond with his money. He returned back to the India.There was no flight in that days because Aeroplane was not found at that time. So he decided to go in ship in sea travel.Travelling in ship take more than one week. So He thought if he was showing his talent in ship he can also earn more money so He started to show his talent in ship also. All peoples in ship also saw his talent and they enjoyed. At that time He thought to show his talent by his Diamond. So He took out his Diamond and showed that beautifullness. All people was seeing with mouth opening. Then He throwed it on the sky and catched it. It was very beautiful because rainbow colors came out in Diamond. Peoples are mesmerized and enjoyed well. So he tryed to throw the Diamond more higher on the sky. But Suddenly ship shaked and Diamond fell down in the deep sea. No one can take it because it was so deep like two mountains deep. So Drama Artist fedup and went to his bed with sad face.

The Moral

From this story what we learn is ,Drama Artist lose his valuable Diamond in deep sea,like him we should not lose his soul. Because soul is very important for everybody. If we do the sin and lose our soul in satan, our life is waste. we have to keep the soul with out sin. But nothing is impossible by the God. we have to get help from the God to keep our soul with out sin by prayers. God bless you.
